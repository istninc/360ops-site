Privilege & Compliance

Designed for
Attorney-Client Privilege.

360ops treats every document, communication, and matter as potentially privileged — because in legal, the cost of a mistake isn't a bug report. It's a bar complaint.

✓
No data stored on 360ops servers — documents processed in-memory only. We never retain client files.
✓
Matter-scoped RBAC — staff only see matters they're assigned to. Paralegals can't access partner-only files.
✓
Privilege detection — AI flags potentially privileged content before processing. You decide what gets analyzed.
✓
Dedicated database — your client data is isolated from all other law firms. Cross-firm data exposure is architecturally impossible.
