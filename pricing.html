FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you launch.

Is there a free trial?
Yes — 30 days with Tier 1 features, fully unlocked. No credit card required. Start building with real AI agents immediately, then decide if 360ops is the right fit for your team.
What's the AI credits wallet?
The credits wallet is completely separate from your subscription. You add credits upfront ($50, $100, $250, or a custom amount), and they're consumed as your agents run AI queries. You get alerts at 50%, 20%, and 0% balance. When your wallet hits $0, AI pauses automatically — there are no surprise charges, ever.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes. You can change your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, so you keep access through the period you've paid for.
Is my data safe?
Every organization gets a dedicated Supabase database — no shared databases, ever. Our connectors are read-only by default; we never write to, store, or cache your files or emails on our servers. Your data stays in your systems. We connect to it; we don't copy it.
What integrations are supported?
Current supported integrations include Microsoft 365 (OneDrive, Outlook, Teams), QuickBooks, Atera, Slack, Google Workspace, and HubSpot. We add new connectors regularly based on customer demand — if you need something specific, reach out and we'll prioritize it.
What's FoundersOS?
FoundersOS is our Tier 4 experience — the full business operating system powered by AI. It starts with Genesis, our conversational onboarding flow that designs your entire AI fleet based on how your business actually works. Agents collaborate across every department, share memory, delegate tasks, and surface intelligence you didn't know you were missing.
Do I need technical setup?
Tier 1 requires zero technical setup — sign up, connect your Microsoft 365 account via OAuth, and your agents are live. Tier 2 and above add additional connectors, each with a simple 5-minute OAuth setup. No code, no APIs, no IT tickets required.
What's Tier 5 Enterprise?
Tier 5 is our Enterprise tier for organizations with dedicated infrastructure requirements. It includes a dedicated Railway deployment (fully isolated), HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA), custom SLA with defined response times, white-glove onboarding, and a dedicated Customer Success Manager. Contact us at hello@360ops.ai for custom pricing.
