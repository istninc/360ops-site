The Problem With Raw AI

Why raw AI tools aren't
enough for business.

Pasting sensitive data into a chat window isn't a strategy. It's a liability. Here's what you're actually risking — and how 360ops solves it.

Problem 01
No Compliance

ChatGPT, Copilot, and raw AI tools see your actual data — PII, financials, client records, internal documents. You're one paste away from a HIPAA, SOC 2, or GDPR violation you don't even know about.

🛡️
360ops Fix
Every query passes through a 12-stage compliance gatekeeper. PII is masked, sanitised, and never sent raw to any AI model.
Problem 02
No Memory

Every ChatGPT conversation starts at zero. Your AI doesn't know your org structure, your clients, your processes, or your history. You're re-explaining context every single time — and burning hours doing it.

🧠
360ops Fix
Agents maintain a persistent, growing memory of your business. They learn over time — your workflows, your clients, your preferences.
Problem 03
No Role Context

One AI for everything means expert-at-nothing. A generic chatbot can't act as your Ops Lead, your Finance Analyst, and your Customer Success Manager simultaneously — with the right context, authority, and guardrails for each.

🤖
360ops Fix
360ops provisions a dedicated agent per role — each trained on your org's data, scoped to its function, and operating within defined compliance bounds.
